Fire Protection Consultants

Protec Group is a consortium of fire protection consultants specializing in the engineering and construction of fire protection systems for mining and metal processing plants. We particularly specialize with Solvent Extraction plants in hydrometallurgy.

Protect Equipment Inc.

Protect Equipment Inc. is the lead consultant of Protec Engineering Group. Providing solutions with risk assessment, scope definition, and investigations of plants in the mining and metal industry.
